How to make Pancakes

Shrove Tuesday sees thousands of people making pancakes, but instead of buying pre-made pancakes follow this recipe for added fun (can you do a double flip?) and extra delicious pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 100g Plain Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 300ml Milk
  • oil

Method

  1. Weigh the flour into a small bowl and make a well in the centre.
  2. In a jug whisk the milk and egg together.
  3. Slowly add the milk and egg to the flour stirring as you do so. Add only enough to make a thick paste initially and stir until there are no lumps.
  4. Heat a little oil in a frying pan running around the base and sides. When the pan is hot pour off any excess oil.
  5. Pour in just enough batter to thinly coat the base of the pan.
  6. Once golden brown on the underside flip and continue to cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve with a topping of your choice.
